My Orders

To view your orders (if the order is placed from the website), then you should follow these steps:

  1. Firstly, click on my account from the Nephot website. Then, log in to your account.
  2. Click on “My Orders” and view the order list.
  3. You can view all of your orders listed. You can track the deliveries or you can even cancel your order.
